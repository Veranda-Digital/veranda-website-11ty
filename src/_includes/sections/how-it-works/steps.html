Delivery, Reimagined

Four Simple Steps To Effortless Deliveries

Veranda removes the guesswork, the missed deliveries, and the stress of waiting around. Here is how the Veranda system brings your groceries and packages home safely, any time of day.

01

Order As You Always Do

Shop with your favourite grocers and retailers. No new apps to learn and no change to your routine. When you check out, simply choose Veranda as your delivery destination.

02

We Deliver On Your Schedule

Couriers securely place your order inside the Veranda Box, day or night, with no delivery window required. No more rushing home or coordinating drop-off times.

03

Kept Fresh & Secure

The Veranda Box keeps perishables at the right temperature and protects every delivery from theft and the elements, so your groceries stay fresh until you are ready for them.

04

Retrieve At Your Convenience

Get a notification the moment a delivery arrives, then unlock the box from the Veranda Dashboard whenever it suits you. Effortless, secure, and always on your time.

Getting a delivery should be as easy as ordering one.
