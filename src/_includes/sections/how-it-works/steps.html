The Smarter Way To

Get Deliveries Into Your Hands

Setting up Veranda takes minutes. From there, every delivery finds its way home safely on your schedule, not the courier's.

01

Set Up Your Box and App

Install your Veranda Box, download the Veranda App and create your account. It only takes a few minutes, and you're ready for your first delivery.

02

Order As You Always Do

Veranda partners deliver straight to your box automatically. Ordering somewhere else? Just add a note in your delivery instructions asking the courier to leave it in your Veranda Box.

03

You Decide When It Opens

For non-partner deliveries, schedule your box to unlock during the delivery window, or set it to stay open and relock itself the moment a drop-off is made.

04

Retrieve At Your Convenience

Get notified when something arrives, then open the box hands-free whenever it suits you. No rushing home, no missed deliveries.
