Core Team

Nic Durish

Chief Executive Officer

Nic is a digital strategist, software developer, and leader with over a decade of experience leading teams in designing and building practical technologies across academic, public-sector, and commercial environments. His work has ranged from studying the Digital Divide in rural communities, to developing accessible software for local governments, to building scalable cloud infrastructures for large enterprises.

As CEO of Veranda, Nic leads the company’s product vision, technical strategy, and long-term growth. He is committed to building practical, accessible technology that improves everyday logistics for households, partners, and communities.

Joshua Wong

Chief Technology Officer

Josh is a technology executive, RF systems expert, and hardware innovator with deep experience in wireless communications, antenna design, and connected-device engineering. He previously served as Manager of the Antenna Design Team at BlackBerry, where he led engineering work on antenna systems for BlackBerry smartphones and tablets.

As Veranda’s Chief Technology Officer, Josh leads the company’s hardware architecture, embedded systems strategy, and wireless communications development. He is committed to building reliable, secure, and practical technology that connects the physical world to modern digital infrastructure.

Dion Dawson

Chief Communications Officer

Dion is a social entrepreneur, technology executive, and communications strategist with deep experience building organizations at the intersection of logistics, food accessibility, and social impact. He is the Founder and Chief Dreamer of Dion’s Chicago Dream and CEO of Cosmic Crate, with work recognized by the White House, Crain’s Chicago Business, the Chicago Innovation Awards, and more.

As Veranda’s Chief Communications Officer, Dion helps shape the company’s public narrative, partnerships, and market presence. He brings a strong belief that enduring companies should create both economic value and meaningful public good.

Patrick Houlding

Director of Software Development

Patrick Houlding is an experienced software engineer with a background in building applications, cloud-connected systems, and resilient networking technologies. His previous work has included developing user-focused mobile apps, improving system reliability through test-driven development, as well as researching and building Mobile Ad-Hoc Network technologies.

As Director of Software Development, Patrick leads the work on our mobile app, cloud services, and partner-facing systems. He is committed to building dependable, thoughtfully designed software that make complex systems feel simple, secure, and effortless to use.
