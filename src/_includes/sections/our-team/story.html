Our Team is

Built To Deliver

Veranda began with a simple frustration shared by its founders: getting a delivery should be as easy as ordering one. Missed deliveries, stolen packages, and spoiled groceries were problems everyone accepted as normal. We did not.
Founded by a team of technologists and operators, Veranda set out to reimagine last-mile delivery from the ground up. The result is the Veranda system, a secure, sustainable, and effortless way to receive anything you order, any time of day, without waiting around.
Today we are proud to be partnering with organizations that share our mission, bringing reliable and accessible delivery to communities that need it most.
