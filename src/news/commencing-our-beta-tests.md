        <div class="pb-10 text-press text-white text-opacity-80 ztablet:text-left ztablet:text-press">
            Veranda is pleased to announce the commencement of beta testing for the Veranda system. Starting this month, a select group of households will
            receive and use Veranda units in their daily lives—accepting grocery and package deliveries on their own schedules, without waiting at home for a
            driver or worrying about what happens after the doorbell rings.
        </div>
        <!-- ABOUT THE BETA -->
        <h4 class="pt-6 pb-4 text-left font-sansmd text-lg font-medium leading-[1.2] tracking-tight text-brightgreen ztablet:text-lg">About the Beta Program</h4>
        <div class="pb-10 text-press text-white text-opacity-80 ztablet:text-left ztablet:text-press">
            The beta program is designed to test the full delivery experience end to end: installation, daily use, carrier handoffs, temperature-sensitive
            groceries, and the Veranda app. Participants will provide structured feedback at regular intervals, helping our engineering and operations teams
            prioritize improvements before we scale to additional markets.
        </div>

        <!-- WHAT WE ARE TESTING -->
        <div class="flex h-auto flex-col items-center justify-between pb-6 zdesktop:flex-row zdesktop:gap-[6em] zdesktop:pb-0">
            <!-- Text Column -->
            <div class="flex flex-[1.1] flex-col items-start justify-start">
                <h4 class="pt-6 pb-4 text-left font-sansmd text-lg font-medium leading-[1.2] tracking-tight text-brightgreen ztablet:text-lg">
                    What We Are Testing
                </h4>
                <div class="pb-10 text-press text-white text-opacity-80 ztablet:text-left ztablet:text-press">
                    Beta testing will focus on reliability, ease of use, and integration with existing delivery workflows. Key areas include secure package storage,
                    grocery freshness over extended windows, notification and tracking in the Veranda app, and the installation experience for households with different
                    porch and entryway configurations.
                </div>
            </div>
